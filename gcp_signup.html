Google Cloud Skills Boost Platform

 

 

Introduction

Cloud Skills Boost (formerley known as Qwiklabs) is a learning platform that has deep integration with the Google Cloud Platform. As a student enrolled on a specific modules in SoCS, you have been given access to the service to carry out one or more of the Cloud Skills Boost hands-on labs.

When you carry out any lab exercise, please always remember that you must use the temporary GCP login details that are listed to the left of each lab - do not login using your own GCP/gmail account details!

Registering and Signing in to Cloud Skills Boost

You will need to create a account here by using your university email address - do not use the "Signin with Google"option as only your university email has been authorised for access.

 

With your account setup using your university email address, it will automatically be added to a share group that gives students access to free credits. Please note you will see a credits value that may be in the 1000s, note this is the total pool of credits for all students to use, so dont be alarmed if you notice this value going down more than the credits you have personally used.
